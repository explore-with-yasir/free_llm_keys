## üÜì Free Access Providers

### **Google AI Studio**  
https://aistudio.google.com

Provides free access to **Gemini** and **Gemma**.

| Model | Daily / Request Limits |
|------|------------------------|
| Gemini 2.5 Pro | ~3M tokens/day |
| Gemini 2.5 Flash | 250 requests/day |
| Gemini 2.5 Flash-Lite | 1,000 requests/day |
| Gemma 3 Series | 14,400 requests/day |

> Note: Data may be used for training outside UK/CH/EEA/EU.

---

### **Cohere**  
https://cohere.com

- **Limit:** 20 requests/min, 1,000 requests/month
- Includes: `command`, `aya`, vision/reasoning variants

---

### **GitHub Models**  
https://github.com/marketplace/models

- Runs behind GitHub Copilot quotas
- Strict limits but good for prototyping

---

### **Cloudflare Workers AI**  
https://developers.cloudflare.com/workers-ai

- **Free:** 10,000 neurons/day
- Supports Llama, Qwen, Gemma, Mistral, DeepSeek

---

### **Google Cloud Vertex AI**  
https://console.cloud.google.com/vertex-ai/model-garden

- Requires billing verification
- Often usable with **trial credits** (see below)

---

### **Vercel AI Gateway**  
https://vercel.com/docs/ai-gateway

- Routes multiple model providers behind one endpoint
- **Free tier:** $5/month usage

---

### **Cerebras Cloud**  
https://cloud.cerebras.ai/

- Generous token & request allowances
- Includes GPT-OSS, Llama, Qwen, etc.

---

### **Groq**  
https://console.groq.com

- Ultra-fast inference
- Very generous daily usage for **Llama** and **Whisper**

---

### **OpenRouter**  
https://openrouter.ai

A gateway for accessing multiple models under shared quota.

**Rate Limits:**
- 20 requests/min
- 50 requests/day  
(Up to **1000/day** with a $10 one-time credit top-up.)

**Free Models Example:**  
Gemma, Llama 3/3.1/3.2/3.3, Mistral, DeepSeek R1, Qwen, Dolphin, Hermes, Kimi, etc.

---

### **NVIDIA NIM**  
https://build.nvidia.com/explore/discover

- Requires phone verification.
- Good for **low-latency GPU inference**.
- **Limit:** ~40 requests/min.

---

### **Mistral (La Plateforme)**  
https://console.mistral.ai/

- Free ‚ÄúExperiment‚Äù tier
- Phone verification required
- Opt-in training data usage
- **Limit:** ~1 request/sec + generous monthly tokens

---

### **Codestral (Code Models)**  
https://codestral.mistral.ai/

Mistral's **code-optimized** LLM.

**Limit:** 30 requests/min, 2,000/day.

---

### **HuggingFace Inference Providers**  
https://huggingface.co/docs/inference-providers/en/index

- Serverless inference  
- Most models < 10GB supported  
- **Credit:** ~$0.10/month free usage
